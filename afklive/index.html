About AFK Live 2025

AFK Live is a 1 day LAN/tournament hosted by yours truly with various different prizes/awards.

In person gaming is way more fun than online so I wanna support that to the fullest. Basically, my house will be an open game fest for the first half and then will be focused on tournament matches for the remaining half.

There'll be 2-3 setups each with a switch + laptop so that both area of games will be available. All setups will be streamed, so make sure to try and get a crazy clip for an award.
